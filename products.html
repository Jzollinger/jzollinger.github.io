Products

nfuse products are based on a patented magnesium permeation technology that optimizes magnesium absorption into the body. Magnesium is essential for overall health and is critical to muscle recovery, improving sleep, energy production and reducing stress.

Despite its importance to the body, most of us do not get enough magnesium through our diets. nfuse skincare products deliver optimal performance that you can trust and also have the added health benefits of magnesium.

NFUSE DEODORANTS

At nfuse, we treat your underarms as a pipeline. The underarms are proven to have one of the highest rates of absorption of chemicals through the skin. Instead of using aluminum and harmful chemicals, nfuse deodorant delivers magnesium, a critical nutrient which your body needs to perform at its best.

NFUSE LOTION

The skin is the largest organ of the body. So, feed your skin the natural goodness you deserve. Anti-inflammatory and deeply healing, nfuse magnesium lotion is specifically formulated to deliver active hydration for smooth, glowing skin and optimize magnesium absorption for whole body wellness.
