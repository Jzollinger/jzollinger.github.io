Why Magnesium?

Magnesium, a naturally occurring mineral, is an essential nutrient for all living organisms. With every use, a healthy dose of magnesium is applied to your body.

Magnesium affects how every part of your body functions:

Boosts Energy

Aids in production, storage and transport of energy. Studies have shown that magnesium elevates energy levels and enables athletes to train longer and harder.

Protects the Heart

Supports and maintains normal blood pressure and heart beat.

Maintains Optimal Muscle Performance

Regulates muscle contraction and relaxation.

Fosters Healthy Brain Activity

Helps transmit and receive nerve signals and regulates over-excitability of nerves.

Improves Sleep

Relaxes muscles, calms nerves, stimulates melatonin synthesis and regulates key hormones for a deep restful sleep.

Reduces Stress

Regulates and controls the release of stress hormones.

Detox

Protects the body from harmful toxins and the brain from the deposit of heavy metals like aluminum, lead, and mercury.

Therapeutic Skin Repair

Improves cellular turnover, helps soften and heal dry, damaged skin and can reduce breakouts and other skin disorders by lowering cortisol levels.
